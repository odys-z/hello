Triangle Printout
=================

.. math::

    \begin{array}{cl}
    \hline
    f(n) & = n ⋅ f(n - 1) \\
    \hline
    5! & = 5 ⋅ f(4)_? \\
       & = 5 ⋅ 4 ⋅ f(3)_? \\
       & = 5 ⋅ 4 ⋅ 3 ⋅ f(2)_? \\
       & = 5 ⋅ 4 ⋅ 3 ⋅ 2 ⋅ f(1)_? \\
       & = 5 ⋅ 4 ⋅ 3 ⋅ 2 ⋅ 1 ⋅ f(0)_? \\
       & = 5 ⋅ 4 ⋅ 3 ⋅ 2 ⋅ 1 ⋅ 1 \\
       & = 5 ⋅ 4 ⋅ 3 ⋅ 2 ⋅ 1 \\
       & = 5 ⋅ 4 ⋅ 3 ⋅ 2 \\
       & = 5 ⋅ 4 ⋅ 6 \\
       & = 5 ⋅ 24 \\
       & = 120 \\
    \end{array}
..
